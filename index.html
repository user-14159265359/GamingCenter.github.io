CS2

Your CS Rating is a visible measurement of your Counter-Strike performance, and it will determine where you stand on global and regional leaderboards. To get your CS Rating, play matches in the updated Premier mode (our Active Duty Pick-Ban competitive mode) either on your own or with your friends.

Valoran

Set in a near-future Earth, Valorant pits teams of players against each other in intense, tactical combat. With a diverse cast of characters, each possessing unique abilities, and a focus on precise gunplay and strategic gameplay, Valorant has quickly become a popular title in the competitive gaming scene.

Subnautica

In "Subnautica," players embark on a thrilling underwater odyssey, diving into the depths of an alien ocean teeming with wonders and dangers. As they navigate the vast and vibrant underwater world, they must gather resources, construct underwater bases, and fend off hostile creatures to survive.

Call of Duty: Modern Warfare

redefines the modern military shooter genre with its intense and gritty depiction of contemporary warfare. Set in a world plagued by terrorism and geopolitical turmoil, players are thrust into the heart of the action as they navigate through a morally complex narrative filled with twists and turns.

The Forest

a survival horror game developed and published by Endnight Games. Set on a mysterious and hauntingly beautiful island, players find themselves stranded after surviving a plane crash. However, they soon discover that they are not alone on the island, as it is inhabited by a cannibalistic tribe of mutants.

Grand Theft Auto V

(GTA 5) is an action-adventure game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. Set in the sprawling metropolis of Los Santos and the surrounding countryside of San Andreas, GTA 5 offers players an open-world playground of unprecedented scale and detail.
